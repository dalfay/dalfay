How It Works

A simple process for people who want professional help exporting a vehicle overseas.

1

Submit Inquiry

Start by telling us who the vehicle is for, where it is going, and whether you already own a car or still need help finding one.

2

Consultation

We speak with you directly to understand the family situation, the destination, local concerns, and the most practical vehicle path.

3

Vehicle and Export Plan

We either work around the vehicle you already own or help you source one, then organize the export path based on the agreed plan.

4

Destination Completion

The vehicle is completed at destination through self-clearing, a referred clearing agent, or a guided structure depending on the case.

Flexible Completion

Different families prefer different levels of support once the vehicle reaches destination.

Clear it yourself

If your family already understands how destination pickup works, they may be able to handle the final clearing and pickup process directly using the required release details.

Use a clearing agent

If the destination side needs outside help, Dalfay can guide you toward a structure that uses a clearing agent or another practical completion path based on the destination.
