B2B Imports

Import solutions for dealerships, mechanic shops, repair businesses, and rental operators sourcing vehicles from the United States.

Dalfay helps overseas vehicle businesses access U.S. cars through a custom consultation-first model that combines sourcing, logistics, export coordination, and flexible completion options tailored to the customer’s market.

Overview

A serious import partnership for businesses that need more than a shipping company.

What this service is

This service is built for overseas businesses that want reliable access to vehicles from the United States. Instead of giving you a generic price sheet and leaving you on your own, we study your business, understand your market, and build a working import relationship around your exact needs.

  • Designed for professional vehicle businesses, not casual buyers
  • Custom quote per vehicle and per situation
  • Consultation-driven from the start
  • Flexible sourcing, transport, and clearing structure

Who this is for

Dalfay works with businesses that buy, repair, resell, or operate vehicles in their home markets. That includes established dealerships, smaller dealers, mechanic shops, repair businesses, and rental operators looking to build a dependable U.S. sourcing channel.

  • Auto dealerships
  • Mechanic and repair shops
  • Rental car operators
  • Entrepreneurs building local vehicle inventory

1-on-1

Every approved business is handled through direct communication and a dedicated consultation process.

Custom

No public pricing grid. Each vehicle and each route is quoted based on the actual business case.

Flexible

Customers can choose different sourcing, transport, insurance, and destination-clearing approaches.

What We Handle

We support the process from early planning all the way to shipment and destination coordination.

Business consultation and quote building

Every business relationship starts with understanding how you currently operate, what types of customers you serve, what type of inventory performs best in your market, and how Dalfay can become a reliable extension of your sourcing operation.

  • Business discovery call
  • Market and customer discussion
  • Vehicle profile alignment
  • Per-vehicle service quote

Vehicle sourcing and execution

Once aligned, we can source vehicles through U.S. auctions, private parties, and dealerships, depending on the business need. We then coordinate inland transportation, export documentation, and shipping preparation.

  • Auction sourcing
  • Private-party sourcing
  • Dealer sourcing
  • Transport to export point
  • Export documentation support
  • Optional insurance coordination

Our model is built around business flexibility, not rigid rules.

Some customers want us deeply involved from sourcing to shipment. Others already know exactly what vehicle they want and simply need a professional company to acquire, move, and export it properly. Dalfay supports both approaches.

How It Works

A clear process for businesses building a dependable import relationship with Dalfay.

1

Submit Business Inquiry

The business submits an inquiry so we can review the company and understand the region, the need, and the intended buying model.

2

Representative Connects

A Dalfay representative gets in touch to learn how the business currently operates and what type of inventory and support would create the most value.

3

Quote and Execution

We issue a case-by-case quote and, once approved, source or receive the vehicle, arrange transport, and handle export coordination.

4

Destination Completion

The business can clear the vehicle itself, work through a clearing agent, or receive guidance depending on the destination and the agreed structure.

Destination Flexibility

Completion options built around how your business prefers to operate at destination.

Self-clearing option

If your business already understands the destination side and prefers to take over once the vehicle arrives, we can provide the required release details so your team can clear and pick up the vehicle directly.

Guided or referred support

If your business prefers outside assistance at destination, Dalfay can coordinate around that structure and help point you toward a practical completion path based on the market and your operating setup.
