Our Story

From a small business vision to a company serving customers around the world.

Why Dalfay was founded

Dalfay was founded in 2018 with a simple but powerful idea: make better vehicles more accessible to more people. In many parts of the world, businesses and families depend on imported vehicles because local manufacturing is limited or unavailable. We saw the need for a company that could do more than list cars. We built a company that could advise, source, coordinate, and execute.

What makes us different

Dalfay is not a generic shipping website and not just a marketplace. We are a consultation-first vehicle access company. We learn what the customer actually needs, source the right vehicle when needed, coordinate transportation, manage export-related steps, and provide flexible options through delivery, pickup, or destination clearing support.

We built Dalfay to connect people, businesses, and markets through better vehicle access.

We believe good vehicles should not be wasted. We believe entrepreneurs, repair businesses, dealerships, and families should have better access to U.S. vehicles. We believe a serious company should combine operational strength with real flexibility. That is the model behind Dalfay.

How It Works

Every relationship starts with consultation and ends with a tailored solution.

1

Consultation

Customers begin by submitting a consultation request so we can understand goals, location, vehicle type, and the level of support needed.

2

Custom Quote

We do not publish generic pricing. Every case is quoted based on sourcing method, route, destination, and complexity.

3

Execution

We source or receive the vehicle, arrange transportation, handle export-related steps, and coordinate the process according to the customer’s case.

4

Flexible Completion

Customers may self-clear, work with a referred clearing agent, receive door-to-door delivery, or pick up locally when available.

Auction Warriors is the sourcing backbone behind our vehicle access model.

Across all three service lines, Dalfay leverages Auction Warriors as a core sourcing channel by default. That gives our customers stronger inventory access while keeping the relationship, strategy, consulting, and overall experience centered on Dalfay.
